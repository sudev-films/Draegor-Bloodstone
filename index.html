About the Project

Developed entirely in Unreal Engine, Draegor Bloodstone is a cinematic journey into the heart of an anti-hero. By bridging the gap between ancient Norse, Greek, and Hindu lore, this project explores the evolution of a god-like being in a high-fidelity digital world.
